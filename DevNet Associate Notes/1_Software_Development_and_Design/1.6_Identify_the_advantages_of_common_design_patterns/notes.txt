Singleton
- Pattern that restricts classes to only being instantiated once during the execution of the program.

Observer
- A pattern where dependent objects are updated or modified by one or more subject objects
- Two classes: subject and Observer
    - Subject: attach, detach and modify
    - Observer: update

MVC (Model View Controller)
- Abstraction where responsibilities are divided into three loosely coupled components
    - Model: Component that stores data
    - View: Component that displays data
    - Controller: Component that handles logic and directs model and View
